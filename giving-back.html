Giving Back

Vortex is all about giving back.

That’s why we’re determined to integrate charities as much as possible. While we are grateful for not only being able to exercise in such an incredible facility and to have ‘able’ bodies and minds to exercise, we understand that not everyone has that luxury.

Our Athletes Care

Vortex is the only fitness studio in the world where every Athlete has a charity of choice in which the revenue of at least 1 class per month will go to their selected charity.

Are you an existing Vortex customer and want to your charity involved? We’d love to hear from you and understand more!

Ride

Our signature 45-minute RIDE is challenges the status quo of regular indoor cycling. Each RIDE is a full-body workout complete with hand weights and core exercises, designed to reboot and strengthen your body. Added movements will work your shoulders, triceps, biceps, abs and oblique’s all while your lower body is continuously challenged by the tempo. RIDE is an intense cardiovascular and toning workout strengthened by the power of music and our unique team of ATHLETES to influence the integrity of your workout.

About

Vortex is Houston’s newest luxury boutique fitness studio.

The brand ethos is simple; pure, no holds barred, un-adulterated fitness against a back drop of absolute luxury. With two workout concepts specially curated by some of the world’s leading fitness experts from London & NYC.

The Perfect Space

The fitness concepts are specifically designed to complement each other. We’ve designed the perfect space for you to come in and smash a workout, become part of our VTX community and embed yourself in the culture that is Vortex. We have a team of hardcode Athletes to inspire and coach you to your goals.

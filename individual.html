Conor

After trying his hand at the "corporate grind," Conor relocated to LA where he happened to come upon SoulCycle one morning. It only took one class for Conor to realize that his true calling was doing something in the fitness industry. Riding like a mad man, Conor decided to bring his Soul to SoulCycle and moved back from the West Coast to open Soul GWCH. You can find him "rocking the suburbs" or "tearing down downtown!" He wont stop until everyone understands that they can become the best version of themselves with every pedal stroke.

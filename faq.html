Do I need to bring anything with me?

We provide everything you need so just come along with your workout kit and you're ready to go. You are of course welcome to bring your own gloves, wraps, cleats, towels, water etc. if you'd rather not use ours.

Do I need to bring cleats for the Ride class?

You can if you like, but we have them ready for you in all sizes at the studio.The cleat type is SPD if you'd like to bring your own.

Are you a franchise or independently owned business?

Are you recruiting?

We’re always on the hunt for great talent. We want Athletes, front of house and cleaning staff. If you think you suit our brand and have value to bring, please contact recruitment@vortex.com

Can I come in and train without attending a class?

No, we’re group exercise only. It’s the best way to train. Pushing you much harder than a regular gym and at a fraction of the price of a personal trainer.

Do I need to bring my own gloves to boxing?

You’re welcome to, but we supply gloves & wraps. We also have an assortment of gloves for sale if you’d like your own.

I’m new to fitness… help!

No problem at all. Vortex caters for all fitness levels. During the class our Athletes will instruct ‘beginner, intermediate & advanced’ levels, just keep a listen out and follow the level that suits you. You’re in class to go at your own pace, but all we ask is that you give it your all to really reap the benefits.
